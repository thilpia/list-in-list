    1.Sylveon

    It evolves from Eevee when leveled up knowing a Fairy-type move and having at least two levels of Affection in the 3DS games, or high friendship in Generation VIII.
    It is one of Eevee's final forms, the others being Vaporeon, Jolteon, Flareon, Espeon, Umbreon, Leafeon, and Glaceon.
