    2.Vulpix

    Born with a white tail and growing up, the tip cracks and colors form and increases to six.
    The fur is soft and curly.
    If attacked by an opponent stronger than him, he pretends to be hurt and runs away.
    Fireworks burn in the body, and when the temperature rises during the day, the body temperature rises and emits flames from the mouth.
    It controls it and uses it like a bulge, and when it is on the verge of evolution, all six tails emit heat as if they were burning.
    It evolves into a nine-tail.
